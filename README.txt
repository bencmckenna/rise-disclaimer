To add disclaimer:
1. Unzip your Rise course
2. Copy the disclaimer.js & disclaimer.txt files to [RISE COURSE]\scormcontent\ subfolder (there should be an index.html in this folder)
3. Open disclaimer.txt in a text editor, change the text to match your disclaimer & save
3. Open index.html in Notepad and add the following line to the <head> tags:
	
	<script type="text/javascript" src="disclaimer.js"></script>
	
4. Rezip your course and upload to the LMS
